<p><p>The water-based pigments market is primarily segmented into organic and inorganic pigments. Organic water-based pigments are derived from natural sources or synthesized carbon compounds, known for their vibrant colors and excellent transparency, making them ideal for applications in arts and crafts, textiles, and cosmetics. In contrast, inorganic water-based pigments are mineral-based, offering durability and stability, often used in industrial applications such as coatings, construction materials, and plastics due to their excellent lightfastness and opacity.</p></p>
