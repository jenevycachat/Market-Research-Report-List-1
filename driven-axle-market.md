<p><p>Driven axles are classified into two main market types: integral and split. Integral driven axles are designed as a single unit, combining the axle housing and differential, which enhances durability and efficiency. They are often used in heavy-duty vehicles. In contrast, split driven axles consist of separate components, allowing for modularity and easier maintenance. This type is frequently leveraged in lighter vehicles or those requiring customization for specific applications. Each type serves distinct market needs and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
