<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement</li><li>Centrifugal Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air and gas compressor market is primarily divided into two types: positive displacement compressors and centrifugal compressors. Positive displacement compressors, such as rotary and reciprocating types, compress air by reducing its volume, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. In contrast, centrifugal compressors utilize high-speed centrifugal force to move air, making them efficient for large volumes and lower pressure applications. Each type serves specific industrial needs, influencing their demand across various sectors, including manufacturing, oil and gas, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
