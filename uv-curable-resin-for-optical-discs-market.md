<p><ul><li>Radical UV-curable Resin</li><li>Cationic UV-curable Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV curable resin market for optical discs primarily consists of two types: radical UV-curable resins and cationic UV-curable resins. Radical UV-curable resins cure quickly upon exposure to UV light, making them suitable for high-speed production and offering excellent adhesion and hardness. In contrast, cationic UV-curable resins involve a chemical reaction that provides better chemical resistance and durability, though they may cure more slowly. Both types are essential for enhancing the performance and lifespan of optical disc applications.</p></p>
