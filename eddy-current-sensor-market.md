<p><p>The Eddy Current Sensor market consists of two main types: Split Type and Integrated Type. Split Type sensors feature a separate probe and electronics, allowing for flexibility in installation and adaptation to various environments. They are ideal for measuring distances in hard-to-reach areas. Integrated Type sensors combine the sensing element and electronics into a single unit, providing compactness and ease of installation. This type is often used in space-constrained applications, offering streamlined performance with minimal setup.</p></p>
