<p><ul><li>Sprial IQF</li><li>Tunnel IQF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mechanical Individual Quick Freezing (IQF) equipment market comprises mainly two types: Spiral IQF and Tunnel IQF. Spiral IQF uses a continuous conveyor system that spirals products through a freezing chamber, enhancing space efficiency and minimizing handling. It’s ideal for bulk freezing of small items like vegetables and seafood. Tunnel IQF, on the other hand, involves a linear conveyor moving products through a freezing tunnel, providing consistent cooling. It’s suitable for larger items, ensuring even freezing and maintaining product quality.</p></p>
