<p><p>Restaurant accounting software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software operates over the internet, allowing restaurants to access their financial data from anywhere, ensuring real-time updates and collaboration. It typically involves subscription pricing and reduces infrastructure costs. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on the restaurant's hardware, offering complete control over data but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in managing restaurant finances.</p></p>
