<p><p>Channel management software is classified into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software offers flexibility and accessibility, allowing users to manage channels remotely, typically on a subscription model. This type is ideal for businesses seeking scalability and lower upfront costs.
