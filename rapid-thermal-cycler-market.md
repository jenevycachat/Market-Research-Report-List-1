<p><p>The Rapid Thermal Cycler market primarily comprises two main types: Real-time Thermal Cyclers and Gradient Thermal Cyclers. Real-time Thermal Cyclers enable real-time monitoring of PCR amplification, offering precise quantification and faster results for applications like diagnostics and research. In contrast, Gradient Thermal Cyclers allow simultaneous temperature variations across samples, optimizing reaction conditions and enhancing reaction efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in molecular biology and genetic research, catering to diverse needs in laboratories and diagnostics settings.</p></p>
