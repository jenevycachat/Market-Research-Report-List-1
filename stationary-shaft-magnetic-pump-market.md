<p><p>The stationary shaft magnetic pump market features two primary types: single-stage and multistage pumps. Single-stage pumps consist of one impeller, making them suitable for low-flow, high-pressure applications. They offer simplicity, compactness, and efficient fluid transfer for various industries. In contrast, multistage pumps contain multiple impellers, enabling them to achieve higher pressures and flow rates. This design is ideal for applications requiring significant vertical lift or fluid transportation over longer distances, catering to more demanding operational needs.</p></p>
