<p><strong>The Magnetic Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Magnetic Bearing</li><li>Passive Magnetic Bearing</li><li>Hybrid Magnetic Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic bearings market comprises three primary types: Active Magnetic Bearings (AMBs), Passive Magnetic Bearings (PMBs), and Hybrid Magnetic Bearings. AMBs utilize electromagnetic forces to provide precise control and stability, making them ideal for high-speed applications. PMBs rely on permanent magnets to maintain positioning and are simpler but offer less dynamic control. Hybrid Magnetic Bearings combine both technologies, achieving balance between performance and simplicity, suitable for various industrial applications. Together, they enhance system efficiency and reduce mechanical wear.</p></p>
