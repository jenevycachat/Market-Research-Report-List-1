<p><p>The Surgical Instrument Tracking Devices market includes two primary types: Barcode systems and Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) devices. Barcode systems utilize printed codes to track surgical instruments, requiring manual scanning for inventory management. Conversely, RFID technology employs radio waves to automatically identify and track instruments, allowing for real-time monitoring without direct line-of-sight. Both methods enhance surgical efficiency, reduce instrument loss, and ensure compliance with safety protocols in healthcare facilities by providing accurate tracking and inventory management solutions.</p></p>
