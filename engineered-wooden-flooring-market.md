<p><strong>The Engineered Wooden Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three Layers Engineered Wooded Flooring</li><li>Multilayer Engineered Wooded Flooring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engineered wooden flooring market comprises primarily two types: three-layer engineered wooden flooring and multilayer engineered wooden flooring. Three-layer engineered flooring features a top veneer layer of hardwood, a core of plywood or high-density fiberboard, and a backing layer, providing stability and aesthetic appeal. Multilayer engineered flooring consists of several layers of wood, typically with a thicker upper veneer, enhancing durability and reducing expansion and contraction. Both types offer versatility, eco-friendliness, and resistance to moisture, making them popular choices for modern interiors.</p></p>
