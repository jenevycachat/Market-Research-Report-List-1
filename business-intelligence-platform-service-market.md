<p><p>Business Intelligence (BI) platforms and services can be categorized into two main market types: on-premises and on-demand. On-premises BI solutions are installed directly on a company’s servers, providing complete control over data and infrastructure but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, on-demand BI, also known as cloud-based BI, offers flexibility and scalability by hosting services in the cloud, allowing users to access data from anywhere with an internet connection, typically with a subscription model.</p></p>
