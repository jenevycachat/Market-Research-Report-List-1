<p><strong>The Automotive Current Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive current sensor market comprises two primary types: open loop and closed loop sensors. Open loop sensors measure current based on the magnetic field created by the conductor without feedback, making them simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, closed loop sensors utilize feedback mechanisms to achieve higher accuracy and reduced sensitivity to external magnetic fields. While open loop sensors are often suitable for general applications, closed loop sensors are preferred for precise measurements in advanced automotive systems.</p></p>
